(Versión en español al final)

 I am  happy to announce that the release of 0.5.3 version of KLog is
available.

After the release of 0.5.2 (yesterday) I found a bug and I considered to
release a new version of KLog as may include data lost if the operator was
not looking carefully the GUI while using KLog.

The mailing list of KLog is in  Klog-users at lists.berlios.de for discussion
about KLog, wishlist, bug report, ideas, suggestions tips on using, ...

 The main language of the list if English.

 Subscribe at:

                  https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/klog-users

We also have a list for development:

                  https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/klog-devel


 From the Changelog:

February-2010  0.5.3
- Fixed a bug that caused modified QSO not being updated.
- Fixed a bug in the way the band & mode combobox managed the data.

 KLog may have many bugs.
 You can download KLog from::
        http://jaime.robles.es/klog

 You will find the tar.gz sources and the debian package.

 You can see some screenshots in the web.

 The KLog's new-releases email list is created using the addresses of
people I have previously received emails from about KLog. Feel free to ask
me to remove your address if you don't want to be alerted of new KLog
releases.

 Thanks for your help and I hope you find KLog useful.


 73

podría haber pérdida de datos si el operador no miraba con atención la
interfaz gráfica mientras usaba KLog.

La lista de KLog es ( Klog-users at lists.berlios.de) para charlar sobre

trucos sobre  cómo usarlo, ...

 Subscribete en:

                  https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/klog-users

                  https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/klog-devel

 Del fichero Changelog vemos los cambios:

Febrero-2010  0.5.3
- Corregido un fallo que hacía que los QSO modificados no se actualizaran.
- Corregido un fallo en la gestión del selector de modo y banda.
